Experience during CITE WEEK 2023

by Jin Ocillos

First Paragraph

CITE Week is an annual event that brings together all BSIT students to participate in various activities. This year, I had the opportunity to be a part of the event and it was an amazing experience. The week-long event was filled with fun and exciting activities, and I had a great time participating with my friends.



Second Paragraph

One of the highlights of CITE Week was the Mobile Legends Tournament, where I supported my friends who were competing. Even though I'm not a big fan of mobile gaming, I was eager to support my friends and even coached them a little. Seeing them progress and make it to the top three was a great feeling, and it was inspiring to see them work hard to achieve their goals.



Third Paragraph

Aside from the Mobile Legends Tournament, CITE Week also offered other events that catered to different interests. There were coding competitions, and Quiz Bee. I took part in some of these events, and it was a great way to learn new skills and meet new people. The diversity of activities made CITE Week an inclusive event, and everyone was able to find something they enjoyed.



Fourth Paragraph

CITE Week was not only about the activities, but it was also a time for bonding with my fellow BSIT students. I had the chance to connect with other students outside my class and even made new friends. We shared our experiences and ideas, and it was a great way to build a sense of community within the program. The week-long event was tiring, but spending time with like-minded people made it all worth it.



Fifth Paragraph

In conclusion, CITE Week was an incredible experience that I will never forget. The opportunity to participate in different events and support my friends was fulfilling, and it was also a great way to bond with my fellow BSIT students. The event highlighted the importance of camaraderie and provided a platform for us to showcase our skills and talents. I'm looking forward to next year's CITE Week and can't wait to see what new adventures it will bring.
